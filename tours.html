This months featured tour.... Ballon Flights

The $75,000 ballon flights to the edge of space will let you sip a drink on-board at the BAR while soaring 19 miles above Earth.

Galaxy Travel Tours announced plans Tuesday to send people up in a capsule, lifted 19 miles by a high altitude ballon.

The flight would take 90 minutes to go up at about 1000 feet a minute, set sail up high for 2 hours.

TThe luxury capsule will allow passangers to walk around and get adrink from the bar.

The latest space tourism venture aims to send paying customers to the edge of space and give them the same awe-inspiring view that adventure Felix Baumgartrner started down upon - all for $75,000 a ticket.

The selling point is the view of the Earth and seeing its curve. The Venture isn't designed for passengers to experience weightlessness.

Indeed, the flight will be more like that of Red Bull daredevil Felix Baumgartner, the Austrian adventurer who leapt from a ballon 24 miles above the Earth, smashing the sound barrier during his descent and landing safely.
